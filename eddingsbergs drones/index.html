About Eddingsberg

Founded in 2015, Eddingsberg has become Malawi's leading manufacturer of military-grade unmanned aerial vehicles. Our commitment to innovation and excellence has positioned us as a trusted partner in defense technology across Africa.

With state-of-the-art facilities in Lilongwe, we combine cutting-edge technology with local expertise to deliver superior drone solutions that meet the evolving needs of modern defense forces.

Our team of engineers and technicians works tirelessly to ensure each drone meets the highest standards of quality, reliability, and performance.
